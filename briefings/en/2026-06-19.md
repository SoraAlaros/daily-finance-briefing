### 6. **Juneteenth: What the Holiday Commemorates**

**Source:** HISTORY.com ([Original Article](https://www.history.com/articles/what-is-juneteenth))

Juneteenth marks June 19, 1865, the day Union troops arrived in Galveston, Texas, to announce the end of slavery — more than two years after President Lincoln issued the Emancipation Proclamation. Congress designated it a federal holiday in June 2021. For financial markets, Juneteenth on a Friday creates a four-day trading week and a three-day weekend, which historically has been associated with modest pre-holiday position lightening.
