### 2. **Nvidia Enters PC Chip Market with RTX Spark Superchip at Computex 2026**

**Source:** Bloomberg ([Original Article](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-06-01/nvidia-enters-windows-laptop-market-taking-on-intel-and-amd))

CEO Jensen Huang unveiled the RTX Spark — a co-developed Arm-based system-on-chip integrating a Blackwell GPU with a CPU (co-built with MediaTek) — during his keynote at Computex 2026 in Taipei. The chip supports up to 128GB of unified memory and 1 petaflop of FP4 AI compute, enabling on-device large language models without cloud connectivity. Six major OEMs — Microsoft, Dell, HP, ASUS, Lenovo, and MSI — will launch RTX Spark-powered Windows PCs in fall 2026.

Nvidia surged over 6%, Dell rallied more than 10%, and HP gained over 8%, while Intel fell ~4.5% and AMD dropped ~3% as the market reassessed the PC processor competitive landscape.

**Source:** CNBC ([Original Article](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/01/mgm-resorts-barry-diller-people-bid.html))

People Inc. already owns 26.1% of MGM's outstanding common stock. Diller, who sits on MGM's board, said he would recuse himself from board deliberations on the proposal. MGM shares jumped nearly 16% on the news.
