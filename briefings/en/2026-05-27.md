### 1. **Mixed U.S.-Iran Peace Signals Send Brent Crude Down Over 4%**

**Source:** Al Jazeera & Axios ([Original Article](https://www.aljazeera.com/economy/2026/5/25/oil-prices-fall-amid-mixed-signals-on-us-iran-peace-deal))

Brent crude plunged roughly 4.55% to approximately $92.27 per barrel as the Trump administration again sent mixed signals on prospects for a permanent ceasefire with Iran.

Since the war began in late February 2026, Iran has effectively blockaded the Strait of Hormuz, disrupting roughly 14 million barrels per day — about one-fifth of global seaborne oil trade. Analysts warn that even if a deal is struck, markets should expect a three-to-six-month normalization timeline for the roughly 100 million barrels of crude stranded on tankers to reach end buyers.
